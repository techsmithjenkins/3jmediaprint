Return Policy

  • Print Errors: If a mistake occurs due to our design, formatting, or printing process—such as incorrect layout, wrong text, or sizing—we take full responsibility. In such cases, we will offer a free reprint or a full refund. Please report the issue immediately upon receiving the order, along with clear details or images.
  • Damaged Items: All items must be inspected immediately upon delivery or handoff. If there's any visible damage (e.g., scratches, smudges, bends), it must be reported on the spot with photo evidence. Once the item is accepted, we cannot process damage-related claims.
  • Cancellations: You may cancel an order within 1 hour of placing it. After this window, we may have already begun production. In such cases, cancellation may not be possible, or only a partial refund may be offered depending on the stage of the job.
  • Approved Designs: Once you approve a design—either digitally or in person—it is considered final. We do not offer refunds or accept returns for errors that were present in the approved design, including typos, incorrect information, or color variations caused by screen vs. print differences.

For help, contact us with your Order ID and a brief explanation.
